Our Party Helpers & Cleaning Services

FastNew's party helpers and cleaning services are designed to make your event seamless and enjoyable. Our experienced team assists with every aspect of your gathering, from setup to cleanup, so you can focus on your guests and the celebration. We handle the details, big and small, to ensure your venue is organized, welcoming, and spotless before, during, and after your event.

  • Event setup: arranging tables, chairs, decorations, and supplies to create the perfect party atmosphere.
  • Serving and guest assistance: professional helpers to serve food and drinks, assist guests, and keep the event running smoothly.
  • On-spot cleaning: continuous tidying and trash removal during the event for a comfortable environment.
  • After-party cleanup: thorough cleaning of the venue, including sweeping, mopping, and trash disposal.
  • Dishwashing and utensil care: washing dishes, glassware, and utensils so you don't have to worry about the mess.
  • Custom requests: let us know your special needs—our team is flexible and ready to help!

Let FastNew handle the work behind the scenes, so you can enjoy your event to the fullest. We make parties easier, cleaner, and more memorable for everyone involved.
